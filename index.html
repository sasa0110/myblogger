Rangiri Dambulu Viharaya

Previous Next

Sri Lanka is famous throughout the world for its centuries old Buddhist cave temples. There is large number of cave temples built by ancient kings. Out of all of them, over 21 century old Dambulla cave temple is the largest, most visited and the well preserved cave temple in Sri Lanka.

Every time I visit the cave temple, the emptyness and the soullessness of the this precious life reveals to me. Inside it fills with such a void, I'll go to rock top and try to think, what feelings I was undergoing

Yala National Park

Previous Next

As commoners we couldn't explorer the vast beauty of the Yala. We rarely see Sri Lankan Leapords or Bears while travelling in safari vehicles. Most of the time elephants and boars are the one wandering in the open spaces.

Yala has been a true heritage of my Motherland as it take care for most of the endemic living species to Sri Lanka.

Unawatuna Beach

Previous Next

Unawatuna is as famous as Sri Lanka itself, and Valmiki couldn't help writing it on Ramayanaya. It is the most attractive place for the every tourist in Sri Lanka. Unawatuna town is well crowded with tourist during the seasons. As most of the tourist have told, it is not the perfect golden sand and turquoise water beach, but they have fallen in love with it.

But its beauty is threaten by greedy businessman, whom is trying to buy every sand in the beach. We will have to stand for it someday sooner...

Udwalawa National Park

Previous Next

Udawalawe National park is best known for Elephant watching among tourists, because of it water reservoir.But it is not limited its beauty to ephants, protected other wild life such as rusty-spotted cat, fishing cat and Sri Lankan leopard.

It is also a bird watching paradise. Sri Lanka spurfowl, red-faced malkoha, Sri Lanka grey hornbill, brown-capped babbler, and Sri Lanka junglefowl also inhabit the Udawalawe and they are endemic to Sri Lanka.
